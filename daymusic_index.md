# Privacy Policy for DayMusic

**Last Updated: April 7, 2026**

---

## Introduction

Thank you for using DayMusic ("we," "our," "us," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

DayMusic is a local lossless music player that prioritizes your privacy. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.** All your data remains on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account, which only you control.

This Privacy Policy explains:
- What information is stored when you use DayMusic
- How that information is used
- Your rights and control over your data
- How we comply with global privacy regulations

---

## Information We Store

DayMusic stores all data **locally on your device** and optionally in **your personal iCloud account**. We do not have servers and cannot access your data.

### Data Stored on Your Device and iCloud

When you use DayMusic, the following information may be stored:

**Music Library Data:**
- Imported music files (FLAC, ALAC, WAV, AIFF, APE, DSD, MP3, AAC)
- Song metadata (title, artist, album, duration, sample rate, bit depth)
- Playlists you create

**App Preferences:**
- EQ equalizer settings and custom presets
- Theme preference (light/dark/system)
- Language preference (English/Chinese)
- Playback mode (sequential/repeat/shuffle)

**Audio Analysis Results:**
- Audio quality analysis data (Hi-Res/CD/Lossy detection)
- Lossless verification results

---

## How We Use Your Information

All data is used **solely to provide the App's core functionality**:

✅ **Play and organize your music** in your local library
✅ **Save and sync your music files** across your devices via iCloud
✅ **Apply EQ settings** to enhance your listening experience
✅ **Analyze audio quality** to verify lossless file integrity

### What We DO NOT Do

❌ **We do not collect data to our servers** - We have no servers
❌ **We do not sell your data** - Never, to anyone
❌ **We do not use your data for advertising** - No ads in DayMusic
❌ **We do not track you** - No analytics, no tracking across apps or websites
❌ **We do not share your data with third parties** - Your data stays private

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All data is primarily stored on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms (Core Data and the app's Documents directory). This data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Music files are stored in **your personal iCloud account**
- Sync is handled by Apple's iCloud Documents
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- You can disable iCloud sync anytime in the app's Settings

### No Server Storage
We do not operate servers. All data processing happens on your device.

### Data Security
- iOS built-in encryption protects local data
- iCloud uses Apple's security infrastructure
- No data transmitted to third-party servers

## Permissions We Request

DayMusic requests the following permissions to provide functionality. **All permissions are optional** - you can deny them and still use the app with limited features.

### 📁 File Access

**Purpose:** To import music files from your device
**How to control:** Managed through the iOS file picker

**What we do:** Copy selected music files into the app's Documents directory
**What we don't do:** Access files without your explicit selection

### ☁️ iCloud Permission (Optional)

**Purpose:** To sync your music files across your Apple devices
**How to control:** Settings within the app → iCloud Sync

**What we do:** Use Apple's iCloud Documents to sync music files
**What we don't do:** Access your iCloud data or store data on our servers

## Your Rights and Control

You have **complete control** over your data:

✅ **Access:** View all your music and settings within the app
✅ **Modify:** Edit song information, playlists, and settings anytime
✅ **Delete:** Remove individual songs, playlists, or all data
✅ **Control Sync:** Enable or disable iCloud sync anytime

### How to Delete Your Data

**Delete specific songs:** Long press on a song and select "Delete"
**Delete all data:** Uninstall the app from your device

## European Union Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

### Your GDPR Rights

1. **Right to Access:** Request access to your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
3. **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Right to Restriction:** Request limitation of processing
5. **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
6. **Right to Object:** Object to processing of your data
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

### Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on:
- **Your consent** (app permissions you grant)
- **Contractual necessity** (to provide app functionality)

### Data Controller

You are the controller of your own data. All data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not act as a data controller or processor as we have no access to your data.

### Exercising Your GDPR Rights

Since all data is stored locally:
- **Access:** View all data within the app
- **Rectification:** Edit any song information in the app
- **Erasure:** Delete songs/playlists or uninstall the app
- **Data Portability:** Access music files via iTunes/Finder file sharing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke permissions in iOS Settings

**Contact for GDPR Inquiries:**
